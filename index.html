About us

Helping Hand Foundation is a non profit organisation founded in the year 2023. We aim to make a difference in the lives of the less fortunate by assisting & providing donations of basic necessities which they need for survival on a day to day basis, like clothing, groceries, school equipment etc. We are an organisation that believes in kindness, giving and a happy community. Our mission to combat hunger and provide essential clothing to those in need. Founded on the belief that access to basic necessities should be a right, not a privilege, we work tirelessly to bridge the gap between surplus and scarcity, ensuring that everyone can live with dignity and hope. It is in your hands to make a better world for all who live in it.
  • Nelson Mandela

    • DONATION DRIVES

    We Do Food Donations: We collect, store, and distribute healthy, nutritious food to families, shelters, and communities in need. Our food distribution networks work to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry. We Do Clothing Donations: Our clothing drives bring warmth, comfort, and dignity to people experiencing poverty. From winter coats to professional attire, we’re dedicated to meeting a range of clothing needs for people of all ages. Why We’re Different: We pride ourselves on our personalized approach and community focus. Every donation we receive and distribute is done with respect and compassion, ensuring recipients feel valued and respected. By fostering a spirit of kindness and generosity, we’re building stronger, more resilient communities.
